GDBase Data management system - GeoData Base of digital photo images).
You can geocode your digital images, indicate on images several objects and get list of objects, data in XML format and geodata in Keyhole Markup Language (*.kml) format.
The system made by Konstantin A. Gebennikov in State reserve "Bogdinsko-Baskunchaksky", in 2014, when he was a vice-director for scientific work.
